My name is Richmond Ekow Barnes and I was born and raised in Ghana. I come from what is considered here in West Africa as a small family. It’s my mother, my twin sister Rosemond and my little brother Desmond. My father passed away last year. Fashion is the way I express myself, it’s my art form of choice. My modelling career started by chance when I went along for a photo shoot with a friend who is a photographer .

Ekow Richmond Barnes
Writer,editor and Brand/publication creative content Producer

Ekow is inspiring, against all odds, He is easily the most compassionate. Talented, experienced, and collaborative Creative Strategist responsible for strategically-led creative concepts, managing photoshoots for branded content, and crafting editorial content across print and digital platforms.
